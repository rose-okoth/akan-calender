Akan Name Calculator

A simple app that uses your birthday and gender to calculate the day of the week you were born, and what your akan name would be. These names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.

A few notable people in history with Akan names include Kwame Nkrumah, Ghana's first president, and the former UN Secretary Gneral, Kofi Annan.
